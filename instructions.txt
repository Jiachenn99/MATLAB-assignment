The RNG RPG 

Hey there adventurer! To play the game, simply select one of the three available modes, Easy, Medium
or Hard and you will be brought into the game! A selection of 4 numbers will be provided, with
different attacks:

Normal hit: Deals normal damage to the enemy
Critical hit: Deals 2 times the normal damage to the enemy
Normal miss: The enemy deals normal damage to you
Critical miss: The enemy deals two times their normal damage to you

The way to win this game, is to pick numbers you think are hits and deplete the health bar of 
the enemy. Good luck and have fun!
